Frequently Asked Questions

Why won't the jokes load?
Make sure you have an internet connection. The app includes built-in jokes that also work offline.

How do I change the language?
Tap the flag/language button in the top-right corner and select your preferred language.

How do I switch between jokes and riddles?
Use the Jokes / Riddles selector at the top of the screen.

How do I reveal the answer?
Tap the "Tap to reveal the answer" button on the card.
