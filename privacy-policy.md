# Privacy Policy for LaughBox

Last updated: April 19, 2026

## Overview

LaughBox is a joke and riddle app for kids and families. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection

LaughBox does **not** collect, store, or share any personal data. The app does not require account registration or sign-in.

## Internet Usage

The app fetches jokes and riddles from a public GitHub repository (github.com/itsgauravsaxena/LaughBox). No user data is sent or stored during this process.

## Children's Privacy

LaughBox is designed for all ages, including children. We do not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

## Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.
